Installing zenyan

Installing the elixir database (MySQL)

  1. Copy elixir.zip to /webroot/data/data
  2. Unzip elixir.zip

Installing zenyan

  1. Copy zenyan<version>.zip to /webroot/public_html
  2. Unzip zenyan<version>.zip

Installing zenyan builder

  1. Copy zenyan_builder<version>.zip to /webroot/public_html
  2. Unzip zenyan_builder<version>.zip

Configuration

If you are building zenyan to a location other than your C: drive perform
the following step.

  1. Open \webroot\public_html\zenyan097\eConfig\envref.php in a text editor and
    change line #16 $drive as required.
